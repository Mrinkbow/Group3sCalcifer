To understand the beginning of the universe, we must date back to 13.8 billion years ago, when the big bang started. Which was as heavy as 10^54 megatons in TNT equivalent. Meaning ironically, a very big and heavy explosion created more than destroyed. And the radiation from it still continues to exist, its called CMB aka the Cosmic Microwave Background and it is the cooled remnant of the first light.
